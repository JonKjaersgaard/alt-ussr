Known USSR Bugs

This section of the manual lists known bugs in the simulation and how to work around them until they are fixed. To report bugs, please contact the maintainer Ulrik P Schultz.

General bugs

The following bugs are known to exist in the simulator.
  • Mac OS X: the simulator is incompatible with JDK 1.6 (we do not have 64-bit versions of our native libraries) and for versions of eclipse above 3.2 the SWT-AWT bridge must be installed (download from Apple Developer Connection)

ATRON simulation bugs

The following bugs are known to exist in the ATRON simulation.
  • Rubber rings and grids: putting rubber rings on modules cause tightly connected structures to vibrate and become unstable. Solution: avoid modules with rubber rings connecting directly to each other, for example by only having rubber-ring modules present in one orientation in a grid.
